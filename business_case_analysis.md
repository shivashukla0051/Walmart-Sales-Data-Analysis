🧠 Retail Sales Business Case Analysis

📌 Objective
This document outlines key analytical problem statements derived from retail sales data. The goal is to explore transaction patterns, customer behavior, and revenue trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

1. Payment Behavior Analysis
Problem Statement: 
Evaluate how different payment methods contribute to overall transaction volume and sales.

Business Relevance: 
Understanding preferred payment modes helps optimize checkout systems and improve customer experience.

---

2. Category Performance by Branch
**Problem Statement:  
Identify which product categories achieve the highest customer ratings across different store branches.

Business Relevance:  
Supports targeted marketing and inventory planning at a branch level.

---

3. Peak Operational Days
**Problem Statement:
Determine which days of the week generate the highest transaction volume for each branch.

Business Relevance: 
Helps in workforce planning and inventory allocation during peak demand periods.

---

4. Sales Volume by Payment Type
Problem Statement: 
Analyze total quantity of products sold segmented by payment method.

Business Relevance:
Provides insight into purchasing behavior and transaction preferences.

---

5. Customer Ratings Distribution
**Problem Statement:
Examine average, minimum, and maximum ratings for product categories across cities.

Business Relevance: 
Helps identify regional preferences and improve customer satisfaction strategies.

---

 6. Profitability Analysis
Problem Statement:
Evaluate total profit generated by each product category and rank them accordingly.

Business Relevance: 
Enables focus on high-margin categories and strategic pricing decisions.

---

7. Preferred Payment Method per Branch
Problem Statement:
Determine the most frequently used payment method in each store branch.

Business Relevance:
Allows optimization of payment infrastructure and localized strategies.

---

8. Sales Distribution Across Time Slots
Problem Statement:
Analyze how transactions are distributed across different time periods (morning, afternoon, evening).

Business Relevance:
Useful for staff scheduling and managing store operations efficiently.

---

9. Revenue Trend Analysis
Problem Statement:
Identify branches experiencing decline in revenue over time.

Business Relevance:
Helps detect underperforming locations and take corrective actions.

---

📊 Analytical Approach
- SQL-based aggregation and filtering
- Time-based trend analysis
- Grouping and ranking techniques
- Data segmentation across multiple dimensions

---

🎯 Outcome
The above problem statements form the foundation for structured data analysis using SQL and Python, enabling extraction of meaningful business insights from retail datasets.
